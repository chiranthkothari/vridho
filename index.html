How it works

You share holdings, not access.

You type or upload what you own. Vridho values it live and keeps it private. There is no order, transfer or withdrawal path anywhere in the product.

What we ask for

  • The holdings you enter or upload: ticker, units, average price
  • An optional first-invested date, only to compute XIRR
  • Your name and email to keep the account private to you

What we never ask for

  • PAN or Aadhaar
  • Bank account or IFSC
  • Demat or BO number
  • Broker login or password
  • Income-tax portal login

The only market feed is app-level data, never a link to your account. You never connect a brokerage.
